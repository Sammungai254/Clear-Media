Media Booking & Placement

At C360 Brands, we offer media bookings for out-of-home solutions such as billboards, signage, streetpole advertising, and banners. Our experienced account managers ensure seamless execution of media campaigns, providing peace of mind to our clients.

  • Media booking at no extra cost
  • One-stop shop for all out-of-home solutions
  • Personalized account handler
  • Timely execution of campaigns

Media Monitoring

We specialize in monitoring outdoor media such as billboards, streetpoles, and signage to ensure maximum visibility. Our service acts as an external audit of your media, ensuring its visibility and maintenance.

  • Regular maintenance of outdoor media structures
  • Bi-monthly reports on media conditions
